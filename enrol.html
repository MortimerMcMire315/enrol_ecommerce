Email money transfer (known as Interac e-Transfer) is an option if you:

  • have an email address or a mobile number, and
  • are registered for Interac e-Transfer service with your financial institution.

To send an Interac e-Transfer payment:

  1. Log in to your financial institution's online or mobile banking and navigate to Interac e-Transfer menu.

  2. Select the account from which to withdraw the funds.

  3. Add a new recipient using Four Point Learning as the name, services@4pointlearning.ca as the email address, Who is offering ? as the security question, and 4PLearning as the answer.

  4. Select Four Point Learning as the recipient.

  5. Fill in the amount of and enter your name in the message area.

  6. Follow the on-screen instructions to confirm the information and complete the transfer.
